The Crying Man (Home wit Dasses)

Direct Translation: Man Crying

The Crying Man depitcs a man from the Nosticallic, crying. The man depicted is Enomaticilatic (ee-no-matt-eck-ee-lack-tick), a man belived to have been deeply depressed after the destruction of his village during the War of The South (Sown of Conflict)

He and his people overtook a whole army. Using the enviroment for their benifit.

The Tomb of Overheat (Ultu-Temper of Tomth)

Direct Translation: Hot Death, Water Tomb

This tomb contains the heroic soldiers who bravly defended our land from the Trianglish, during the War of the South. Many of these soldiers used heat to there advantage, but lots of them were wounded in battle, and they couldn't move, burning to death in the sun.

Sandfalls (Fown of, Dunds of Miter)

Direct Translation: Sand-Water Falls

This water fall is connected to Cave Dunowrath, a cave once connecting Hotland to Volcanic Island. The water fall, formed after a fatal land slide, destroying villages, and collapsing caves. Now you can only get in through, by jumping in the deep underground lake.
You will not be able to get back up. (Travel to Volcanic Island?)
