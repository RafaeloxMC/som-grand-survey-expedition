Message for Tourists:

The way temerature is messured in Hotland is different than most countrys. In most places, the temperature is messured per area. But in Hotland, it is only messured once, in one location (One with an ITI of 2). Then, it is modified based on the local ITI.

These are 3 main components to how you messure it:

Istemper is the Hotlandic name for Hotland. Its the offical language, its thats why its used here.

  • IT: Istemper Temperature: The temperature of the whole island. This number is modified to the local area. (ILT)
  • ITI: Istemper Temp. Index: Based on the location, it is the modifier of of the temperture. The base IT is messured in an area with 2*ITI
  • ILT: Istemper Local Temperature: The final temperature of the location. This is what can be converted to °C or °F (Celcius is the secondary messurment of temperature in Hotland)
